The Magic Circle

My favorate Processing work.
Due to the fever of magic in fairytales and ACG works, I wished to become an enchanter some day since my childhood. So, I have highly passion to create something “magic”. That’s why I started this “Magic Circle” project right after I had basic knowledge of Processing coding.
Here are some local shots of the project. Every time you click the spacebar, you can gain a new random Magic Circle.
